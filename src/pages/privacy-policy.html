D2 Health Club – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [Jan 14, 2025]

Welcome to D2 Health Club (the "App"), owned and operated by KARTA CREATIVE LTD ("we," "us," or "our"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your data when you use our App.

Our legal bases for processing your data typically include: user consent (e.g., agreeing to this Privacy Policy and creating an account) and contractual necessity (e.g., to provide core App services such as step tracking and in-game rewards).

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

We collect your email address to create and manage your user account. We do not collect other personal details such as full name, date of birth, or phone number. We also do not collect any location data (precise or approximate).

2.2 Health and Fitness Data

The App tracks your daily step goal, daily step count, and total steps since registering. We do not collect other health metrics such as calorie burn, distance traveled, or heart rate.

2.3 Gaming Account Data

If you connect your gaming account (e.g., Roblox) to receive in-game rewards, we collect your Roblox account ID to verify eligibility and deliver rewards. We do not store any other gaming platform credentials, though the authentication process may occur via Apple, Discord, or Roblox.

2.4 Device and Usage Data

We do not currently store any device information (e.g., IP address, operating system) or in-app usage analytics (e.g., screen views, time spent). However, certain runtime operations may require standard device data for the App to function (e.g., for authentication or connecting to third parties), which is not stored in our database.

2.5 Cookies or Similar Technologies

We do not directly use cookies, tracking pixels, or SDKs to collect user behavior data within the App. However, when you authenticate using Apple, Discord, or Roblox, those third-party platforms may process your information according to their own privacy practices.

3. How We Use Your Data

We primarily use your data to:

  • Provide core App functions, such as displaying step counts and leaderboards.
  • Allow you to connect your gaming account and receive in-game rewards (via Roblox ID).
  • Maintain and improve the App (e.g., address bugs or performance issues).
  • Communicate with you regarding updates or changes to our services (if relevant in the future).

We do not currently send marketing emails or push notifications based on your data or behavior, nor do we run remarketing or retargeting campaigns with user data.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Aggregated Data

We may share aggregated or anonymized step data (e.g., total number of steps recorded by users, anonymized stats for top users, or aggregated streak data) with potential investors, business partners, or for general promotional materials.

4.2 Non-Aggregated Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to third parties. However, we may disclose non-aggregated personal data in limited circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to valid legal processes (e.g., law enforcement requests).
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or other business transaction where user information may be transferred as part of the assets.

4.3 International Transfers

Our servers are hosted in the EU, and we strive to keep your data within the EEA. However, certain team members or gaming partners may be located outside the EU/EEA. In such cases, we take reasonable measures to ensure an adequate level of data protection, such as using Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms when necessary.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data (including step history) unless we have a lawful basis to keep it (e.g., aggregated or anonymized data for analytics). We may also periodically delete inactive accounts or associated data to manage storage costs, in line with our internal policies.

6. Data Security

We use Supabase for database and authentication, which employs industry-standard security protocols. Access to user data is restricted to our senior technical team members. We also use common security measures to safeguard data in transit (e.g., HTTPS) and at rest (e.g., encryption at the service provider level). While we strive to protect your data, no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6.1 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will promptly investigate and take appropriate remedial actions. Where required by law, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable regulations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of data processing.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise your rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable laws, please email merlin.bergmann@karta.game. We also provide an in-app account deletion option that automatically deletes your associated data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us at merlin.bergmann@karta.game, and we will take steps to delete the information as soon as possible.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we do, we will post the revised Policy and, if necessary, notify you via email, in-app banner, or push notification. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the latest changes were made.
